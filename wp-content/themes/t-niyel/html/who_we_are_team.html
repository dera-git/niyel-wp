We are Niyel !

Our people

Having a dependable team is essential for success. In other words, team work makes the dream work! We have a diverse and energetic team of consultants from around the world with educational backgrounds spanning over 40 countries and 4 continents. All our experts a creative approach to strategies and tactics, thinking outside the box to solve problems, offer guidance, and find new ways to drive impact. Our team has expertise in many development issues including health, sanitation, migration, environmental justice, education, agriculture, nutrition, citizen participation, political campaigning, and more.
