What are the Flagship Projects ?

Our flagship projects focus on achieving long term goals in major areas of intervention, and allow us to showcase how Niyel drives innovation and change in Africa and around the world. Our projects work to transform systems and manifest efficient and effective policies and practices in various communities, and across sectors. These multi-year initiatives vary in length and scope, however all allow time for research, data compilation, and engaging with decision-makers and civil society on national policies. Most are based on partnerships with our clients, some are grants from non-profits or other organizations—all are meaningful.
